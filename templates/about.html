Welcome to The Third Eye

Hey there, curious minds!
Welcome to The Third Eye
I'm Komal Singh, a budding journalist from the Indian Institute of Mass Communication with a head full of stories and a heart that thrives on capturing the essence of life. This space, The Third Eye, is my creative canvas. Here, I weave together words, visuals, and sounds to explore the depths of life, reality, and everything in between.



What can you expect to find on The Third Eye?
Blogs! Dive into my thoughts and experiences as I tackle themes both profound and playful.
Photography! Witness the world through my lens, where everyday moments transform into stories waiting to be told.
Podcasts! Tune in to my unfiltered conversations about life, reality, and the questions that keep us up at night.



The Third Eye is a platform for me to share my unique perspective and connect with kindred spirits. I believe in the power of stories to bridge divides, spark curiosity, and ignite change. So, grab a cup of chai, settle in, and let's embark on this exploration together! ☕
Stay tuned for fresh content, insightful explorations, and a whole lot of creative expression as this is just the beginning!
